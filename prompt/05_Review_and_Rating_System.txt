Title: Fractional 5-Star Review System

Objective:
Allow users to rate businesses with fractional stars and optional comments.

Instructions:
- Rating input: 0.5 to 5.0 scale
- Store average rating per business
- Display stars visually (half/full)
- Allow one review per user per business
- Include schema.org markup for reviews

Validation:
- Ratings must average correctly
- Reviews must be editable by author
- Rich snippets must validate via Google
