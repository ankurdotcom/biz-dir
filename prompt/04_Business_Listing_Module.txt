Title: Business Listing CRUD and Town Mapping

Objective:
Enable contributors to create, read, update, and delete business listings, mapped to towns.

Instructions:
- Build listing form with fields: name, category, contact, description, town
- Auto-generate SEO-friendly URLs: `/town-name/business-name`
- Include sponsored flag and logic for priority display
- Listings must be searchable and paginated

Validation:
- Listings must be editable only by creator or moderator
- Sponsored listings must appear at top of town pages
- Town pages must show correct listings
